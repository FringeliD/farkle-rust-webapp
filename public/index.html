Rules

Roll six dice. Keep at least one complete scoring selection, then roll again or bank the turn score.

Singles: each 1 scores 100, each 5 scores 50.

Three of a kind scores 1000 for ones, or face value x 100 for twos through sixes. Four, five, and six of a kind multiply that base by 2, 4, and 8.

Special six-dice scores: straight, three pairs, and four of a kind plus a pair score 1500. Two triplets score 2500.

If all six dice score, hot dice lets you roll all six again in the same turn. A Farkle loses the current turn score.
